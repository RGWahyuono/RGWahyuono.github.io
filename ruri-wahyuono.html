Assistant Professor at Department of Engineering Physics, Faculty of Industrial Technology and System Engineering, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) Surabaya, INDONESIA.

Dr. Wahyuono received the B.Eng and M.Eng from the Engineering Physics - ITS. He had the opportunity to join the supramolecular chemistry laboratory at Kumamoto University for his master thesis developing photoactive electrodes for the dye-sensitized solar cells under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hirotaka Ihara. Dr. Wahyuono completed his PhD in Physical Chemistry at Friedrich Schiller University Jena, Germany, working with Prof. Dr. Benjamin Dietzek. He is currently an Assistant Professor with the Department of Engineering Physics - ITS. His research interest is nanomaterials development for energy and environmental applications, and the development of low-cost and flexible biosensors. During leisure time, he enjoys cooking, hiking, playing badminton, and volleyball.
