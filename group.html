ENABLE working group is a part of Laboratory of Advanced Functional Materials, Department of Engineering Physics - ITS. Our working group focuses on the engineering and development of nanomaterials for

  • energy
  • environment
  • biomedical application

covering material selection & characterization, design, and fabrication, system development and optimization. Our group is also interested in developing characterization system combining spectroscopy and electrochemistry techniques to unravel fundamental mechanism of photoactive thin film electrodes.
