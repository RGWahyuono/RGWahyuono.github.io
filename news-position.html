Open Positions

We are constantly looking for students (bachelor or master) who are interested to join & strengthen our team. Currently, we offer several topics for undergraduate and master thesis:

  • Development of Laser Beam induced Spectroelectrocurrent for photoactive electrode characterization.
  • Development of Thermal-Assisted Shearing System for organic-electronic material deposition.
  • Development of Langmuir-Blodgett System for organic-electronic material deposition.
  • Design, dynamic modeling and fabrication of photocatalytic reactor.
  • Synthesis and characterization of catalyst for photocatalytic hydrogen production.
  • Synthesis and characterization of catalyst for photocatalytic pollutant degradation.
  • Development and optimization of paper-based microfluidic biosensors.
  • Mapping renewable energy potential under climate change scheme.
do not hesitate to contact us!
